# § 38-2-1077. Principals

(1) Commits an offense punishable by this article or aids, abets, counsels, commands, or procures its commission; or
(2) Causes an act to be done which if directly performed by that person would be punishable by this article is a principal.

## History

Added by 2015 Ga. Laws 99,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2015.
