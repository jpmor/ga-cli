# § 38-2-1018. General courts-martial

(1) Confinement for a period of not more than ten years;
(2) Restriction to specified limits for not more than six months;
(3) Dismissal, dishonorable discharge, or bad conduct discharge;
(4) Forfeiture of all or a portion of pay and allowances;
(5) Restitution;
(6) Reduction to the lowest or any intermediate pay grade of enlisted persons;
(7) A reprimand; and
(8) No punishment.

## History

Added by 2015 Ga. Laws 99,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2015.
