# § 40-6-360. Rights of persons operating low-speed or multipurpose off-highway vehicles

Every person operating a low-speed vehicle or multipurpose off-highway vehicle shall be granted all the rights and shall be subject to all the duties applicable to the driver of any other vehicle under this chapter except as to special regulations in this part and except as to those provisions of this chapter which by their nature can have no application.

## History

Amended by 2023 Ga. Laws 241,§ 2-7, eff. 7/1/2023.
