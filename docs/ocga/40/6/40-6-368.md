# § 40-6-368. Requirements for streets or highways on which joint use by regular vehicle traffic and PTVs permitted

(1) Have speed limits of 25 miles per hour or less, as established by an engineering and traffic survey; and
(2) Have been determined by a qualified traffic engineer to accommodate personal transportation vehicles without adversely impacting traffic safety or the travel needs of commuters and other users.

## History

Added by 2014 Ga. Laws 629,§ 11, eff. 7/1/2014.
