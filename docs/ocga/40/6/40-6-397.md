# § 40-6-397. Aggressive driving

(a) A person commits the offense of aggressive driving when he or she operates any motor vehicle with the intent to annoy, harass, molest, intimidate, injure, or obstruct another person, including without limitation violating Code Section 40-6-42, 40-6-48, 40-6-49, 40-6-123, 40-6-184, 40-6-312, 40-6-390, or 40-6-390.1 with such intent.
(b) Any person convicted of aggressive driving shall be guilty of a misdemeanor of a high and aggravated nature.

## History

Amended by 2021 Ga. Laws 152,§ 10, eff. 5/3/2021.
Added by 2001 Ga. Laws 161, § 1-7, eff. 7/1/2001.
