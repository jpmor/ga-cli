# § 40-1-167. Required information on license plates of limousines

(1) Limousine carrier name;
(2) City and state of principal domicile;
(3) Company telephone number; and
(4) Motor carrier identification number if the limousine carrier is a commercial motor carrier or motor carrier authorization number issued by the department if the limousine carrier is a lightweight commercial vehicle. The cost for such license plate shall be the sole responsibility of the limousine carrier and must be placed on each certificated vehicle prior to such vehicle being placed in service.
