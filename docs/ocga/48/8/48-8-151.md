# § 48-8-151. Disbursement of proceeds

(1) One percent of the amount collected shall be paid into the general fund of the state treasury to defray the costs of administration; and
(2) The remaining proceeds of the tax shall be distributed to the governing authority of the qualified consolidated government imposing the tax.

## History

Added by 2023 Ga. Laws 22,§ 2, eff. 4/24/2023.
