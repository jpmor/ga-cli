# § 48-8-68. Relief from liability in certain circumstances for failure to collect tax at new rate

(1) The seller collected tax at the immediately preceding effective rate; and
(2) The seller's failure to collect at the newly effective rate does not extend beyond 30 days after the date of enactment of the new rate. The provisions of this Code section do not apply if the commissioner establishes that the seller fraudulently failed to collect at the new rate or solicits purchasers based on the immediately preceding effective rate.

## History

Added by 2010 Ga. Laws 507,§ 18, eff. 7/1/2011.
