# § 48-8-267. Disbursement of proceeds

(1) One percent of the amount collected shall be paid into the general fund of the state treasury in order to defray the costs of administration; and
(2) Except for the percentage provided in paragraph (1) of this Code section, the remaining proceeds of the tax shall be distributed pursuant to the terms of the intergovernmental agreement.
