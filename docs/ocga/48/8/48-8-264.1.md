# § 48-8-264.1. Referendum timing

(1) In odd-numbered years, on the Tuesday after the first Monday in November; or
(2) In even-numbered years:
  (A) On the date of and in conjunction with the presidential preference primary if one is held that year;
  (B) On the date of the general primary; or
  (C) On the Tuesday after the first Monday in November.

## History

Added by 2022 Ga. Laws 752,§ 4, eff. 5/2/2022, app. with respect to taxes imposed or to be imposed under resolutions or ordinances adopted on or after 5/2/2022.
