# § 36-36-132. Unincorporated islands prohibited

There shall be no deannexation under this article that results in the formation of one or more unincorporated islands or in part of the area remaining in the municipal corporation no longer being a contiguous area of such municipal corporation.

## History

Added by 2023 Ga. Laws 257,§ 3, eff. 5/2/2023.
