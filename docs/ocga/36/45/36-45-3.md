# § 36-45-3. Definitions

(1) "Institute" means the Harold F. Holtz Municipal Training Institute.
(2) "Municipal governing authority" means the governing authority of a municipal corporation.
(3) "State" means the State of Georgia and any department, board, bureau, commission, or other agency thereof.
(4) "Vinson Institute" means the Carl Vinson Institute of Government of the University of Georgia.

## History

Amended by 2004 Ga. Laws 779, § 1, eff. 5/19/2004.
