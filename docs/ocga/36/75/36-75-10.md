# § 36-75-10. Construction

This chapter shall be liberally construed to effect the purposes hereof. Sale or issuance of bonds by any authority shall not be subject to regulation under Chapter 5 of Title 10, the "Georgia Uniform Securities Act of 2008," or any other law. No proceeding or publication not required in this chapter shall be necessary to the performance of any act authorized in this chapter, nor shall any such act be subject to referendum.

## History

Amended by 2008 Ga. Laws 528,§ 11, eff. 7/1/2009.
Added by 2003 Ga. Laws 379, § 1, eff. 6/4/2003.
