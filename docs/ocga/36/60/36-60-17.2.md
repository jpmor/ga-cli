# § 36-60-17.2. Limited exemption from storm water fees

Land located within a railroad's right of way and covered with ballast and rail shall be exempt from any fees imposed by any county or municipality for the management, collection, or disposal of storm water; provided, however, that railroad stations, maintenance buildings, or other developed land used for railroad purposes shall not be exempt from storm-water fees.

## History

Added by 2018 Ga. Laws 476,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2018.
