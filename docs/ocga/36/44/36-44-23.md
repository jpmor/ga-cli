# § 36-44-23. Cumulative and supplemental powers

The powers provided by this chapter are intended by the General Assembly to be cumulative and supplemental to any powers heretofore provided by law for counties, municipalities, and consolidated governments of this state and not in lieu of any such heretofore existing powers.

## History

Added by 2009 Ga. Laws 52,§ 2, eff. 4/22/2009.
