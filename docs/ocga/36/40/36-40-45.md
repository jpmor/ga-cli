# § 36-40-45. Payment and use of funds

Funds distributed under this article by the Office of the State Treasurer shall be paid to the municipal corporation in the name of the municipal treasurer or other official or officials authorized to receive municipal funds, as listed in the certificate required by Code Section 36-40-42. Such funds shall be expended by the municipal corporation only for the purposes prescribed in Code Section 36-40-41.

## History

Amended by 2010 Ga. Laws 621,§ 2, eff. 7/1/2010.
