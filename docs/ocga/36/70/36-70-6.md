# § 36-70-6. Promotion of deployment of broadband services by broadband services providers

(a) As used in this Code section, the terms "broadband services" and "broadband services provider" shall have the same meanings as provided in Code Section 50-40-1.
(b) The governing bodies of municipalities and counties shall provide in any comprehensive plan for the promotion of the deployment of broadband services by broadband services providers.

## History

Added by 2018 Ga. Laws 423,§ 3-1, eff. 5/7/2018.
