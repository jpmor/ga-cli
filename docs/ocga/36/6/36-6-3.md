# § 36-6-3. Form of oath

In addition to the oath required of all public officers, before entering on the duties of their office, county treasurers must take the following oath:

"I, __________________, do swear that I will faithfully collect, disburse, and account for all moneys or other effects of the county, and otherwise faithfully discharge all the duties required of me by law as county treasurer. So help me God."
