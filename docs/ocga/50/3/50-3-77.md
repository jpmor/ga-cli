# § 50-3-77. State transportation history museum

(a) The Southeastern Railway Museum in Duluth, Georgia, is designated as the official state transportation history museum.
(b) The Department of Economic Development and other public agencies and leaders in the tourism industry are encouraged to work together to maximize advertising programs which permit citizens of other states and nations to learn of the Southeastern Railway Museum and to visit this state for tourism purposes.

## History

Amended by 2004 Ga. Laws 569, § 27, eff. 7/1/2004.
