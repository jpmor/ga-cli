# § 50-3-2. Pledge of allegiance to state flag

The following is adopted as the pledge of allegiance to the state flag:

"I pledge allegiance to the Georgia flag and to the principles for which it stands: Wisdom, Justice, Moderation, and Courage."

## History

Amended by 2022 Ga. Laws 788,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2022.
