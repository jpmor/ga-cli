# § 50-17-90. Definitions

(1) "Governing body" means, with respect to the state, the Georgia State Financing and Investment Commission, and with respect to a state authority, such authority's board.
(2) "State authority" shall mean "state authority" as defined in paragraph (9) of Code Section 50-17-21.

## History

Added by 2004 Ga. Laws 614, § 7, eff. 5/17/2004.
