# § 50-1-1. Agency mailing lists; updating; restriction on mailing materials to officials no longer in office

(a) For the purpose of this Code section, the term "state agency" means a department, agency, board, commission, or authority of state government.
(b) Each state agency is required to update annually all mailing lists of the agency.
(c) No state agency shall mail publications or materials to a previously elected state officer who is no longer in office except upon written request.
