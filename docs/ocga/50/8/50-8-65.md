# § 50-8-65. Annual report to Board of Community Affairs

(1) The name and address of each contractor, public or private, with which the commission contracted and which received more than a total of $500.00 from the commission; and
(2) The amount of public funds received by the contractor from the commission.

## History

Amended by 2009 Ga. Laws 8,§ 50, eff. 7/1/2009.
