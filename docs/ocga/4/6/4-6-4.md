# § 4-6-4. Licenses - Revocation, cancellation, or suspension

Every licensed dealer and livestock market operator who shall violate this chapter or rules and regulations established by the Commissioner pursuant to this chapter shall have his or her license revoked, canceled, or suspended, upon a notice and hearing.

## History

Amended by 2017 Ga. Laws 42,§ 4, eff. 7/1/2017.
