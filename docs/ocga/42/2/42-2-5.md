# § 42-2-5. Administrative functions of department

(a) The department shall administer the state's correctional institutions and the rehabilitative programs conducted therein.
(b) The department shall provide The Council of Superior Court Clerks of Georgia the data set forth in Code Section 15-12-40.1, without charge and in the electronic format requested.

## History

Amended by 2014 Ga. Laws 578,§ 13, eff. 7/1/2014.
