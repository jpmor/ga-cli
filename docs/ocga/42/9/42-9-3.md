# § 42-9-3. Definitions

(1) "Board" means the State Board of Pardons and Paroles.
(2) "Community supervision officer" means a person who supervises probationers or parolees for the department.
(3) "Department" means the Department of Community Supervision.
(4) "Split sentence" means any felony sentence that includes a term of imprisonment followed by a term of probation.

## History

Amended by 2015 Ga. Laws 73,§ 5-81, eff. 7/1/2015.
