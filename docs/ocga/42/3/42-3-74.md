# § 42-3-74. Judicial approval for pretrial release and diversion program required

No person shall be released on an unsecured judicial release as provided for in Code Section 17-6-12 or approved for a pretrial release and diversion program without first having the approval in writing of the judge of the court having jurisdiction of the case.

## History

Amended by 2020 Ga. Laws 547,§ 3-3, eff. 1/1/2021.
Added by 2015 Ga. Laws 73,§ 1-1, eff. 7/1/2015.
