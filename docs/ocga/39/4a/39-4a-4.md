# § 39-4A-4. [See Note] "Public child placing agency" defined

As defined in Article II of the Interstate Compact for the Placement of Children contained in Code Section 39-4A-7, the term "public child placing agency" means, with reference to this state, the Department of Human Services.

## History

Added by 2024 Ga. Laws 425,§ 1, eff. upon enactment of the Interstate Compact for the Placement of Children into law by the thirty-fifth state.
