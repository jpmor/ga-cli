# § 39-4-9. [For Repeal, See Note] Applicability of Code Section 49-5-15 to placements made pursuant to compact

Code Section 49-5-15 shall not apply to placements made pursuant to the Interstate Compact on the Placement of Children.

## History

Repealed by 2024 Ga. Laws 425,§ 2, eff. upon enactment of the Interstate Compact for the Placement of Children into law by the thirty-fifth state.
