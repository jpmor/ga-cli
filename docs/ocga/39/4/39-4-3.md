# § 39-4-3. [For Repeal, See Note] "Executive head" defined

As used in Article VII of the Interstate Compact on the Placement of Children, the term "executive head" means the Governor.

## History

Repealed by 2024 Ga. Laws 425,§ 2, eff. upon enactment of the Interstate Compact for the Placement of Children into law by the thirty-fifth state.
