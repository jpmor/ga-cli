# § 49-4-165. Notice to consumers

(a) A qualified long-term care insurance partnership policy shall contain a summary notice to the consumer in plain language on the current law pertaining to asset disregard and asset tests.
(b) The notice to the consumer under subsection (a) of this Code section shall be developed by the Commissioner of Insurance.

## History

Amended by 2006 Ga. Laws 505,§ 5, eff. 4/19/2006.
Added by 2005 Ga. Laws 159,§ 1, eff. 5/5/2005.
