# § 53-10-5. Applicability of chapter

This chapter shall not apply in the case of wills, trusts, deeds, contracts of insurance, or any other situation where provision is made for distribution of property different from that provided in this chapter or where provision is made for a presumption as to survivorship that results in a distribution of property different from that provided in this chapter.

## History

Amended by 2020 Ga. Laws 508,§ 1-61, eff. 1/1/2021.
