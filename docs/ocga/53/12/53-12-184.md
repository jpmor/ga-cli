# § 53-12-184. Effect of Code Sections 53-12-180 through 53-12-183 on powers of the courts or the Attorney General

Nothing in Code Sections 53-12-180 through 53-12-183 shall impair the rights and powers of the courts or the Attorney General of this state with respect to any corporation.

## History

Added by 2010 Ga. Laws 506,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2010.
