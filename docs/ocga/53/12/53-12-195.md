# § 53-12-195. Effect of Code Sections 53-12-190 through 53-12-193 on powers of courts and Attorney General

Nothing in Code Sections 53-12-190 through 53-12-193 shall impair the rights and powers of the courts or the Attorney General of this state with respect to any trust.

## History

Added by 2010 Ga. Laws 506,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2010.
