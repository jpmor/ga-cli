# § 53-12-153. Name of trust

If the deed creating a trust estate under Code Section 53-12-151 so provides, the trustee may conduct and transact the affairs of the trust estate under a business or trade name, which name shall be set forth in the deed. The name may include the word "trust" but shall not include the words "trust company."

## History

Added by 2010 Ga. Laws 506,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2010.
