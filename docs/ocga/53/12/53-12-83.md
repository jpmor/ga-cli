# § 53-12-83. Creditors' claims against property that is subject to withdrawal right

The holder of a power of withdrawal, during the period that the power may be exercised, shall be treated in the same manner as the settlor of a revocable trust to the extent of the property subject to the power. The lapse, release, or waiver of a power of withdrawal shall not cause the holder to be treated as a settlor of the trust.

## History

Added by 2010 Ga. Laws 506,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2010.
