# § 53-12-308. Personal liability of trustee

(a) A trustee shall not be personally liable on any warranty made in any conveyance unless the intention to create a personal liability is distinctly expressed.
(b) Unless otherwise provided in the contract, a trustee shall not be personally liable on contracts properly entered into in the trustee's fiduciary capacity unless the trustee fails to reveal the trustee's representative capacity in the contract.
(c) A judgment rendered in an action brought against the trust shall impose no personal liability on the trustee or the beneficiary.

## History

Added by 2010 Ga. Laws 506,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2010.
