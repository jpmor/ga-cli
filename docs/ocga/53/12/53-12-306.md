# § 53-12-306. Action against cotrustee

(a) A trustee may maintain an action against a cotrustee to:
  (1) Compel the cotrustee to perform duties required under the trust;
  (2) Enjoin the cotrustee from committing a breach of trust; or
  (3) Compel the cotrustee to redress a breach of trust committed by such cotrustee.
(b) The provision of remedies for a breach of trust shall not prevent resort to any other appropriate remedy provided by statute or common law.

## History

Added by 2010 Ga. Laws 506,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2010.
