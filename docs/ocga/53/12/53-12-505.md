# § 53-12-505. Relief from duty and liability

A trust instrument may relieve a cotrustee from duty and liability with respect to another cotrustee's exercise or nonexercise of a power of the other cotrustee to the same extent that a directed trustee is relieved from duty and liability with respect to a trust director's power of direction under this article.

## History

Added by 2018 Ga. Laws 366,§ 25, eff. 7/1/2018.
