# § 53-12-43. Power of agent or conservator to revoke trust

(a) A settlor's powers with respect to revocation, amendment, or distribution of trust property may be exercised by an agent under a power of attorney only to the extent expressly authorized by the trust instrument and the power.
(b) A settlor's powers with respect to revocation, amendment, or distribution of trust property may be exercised by the settlor's conservator only as provided in Code Section 29-5-23.

## History

Added by 2010 Ga. Laws 506,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2010.
