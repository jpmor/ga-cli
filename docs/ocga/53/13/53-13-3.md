# § 53-13-3. Application

(a) This chapter shall apply to a:
  (1) Fiduciary acting under a will or power of attorney;
  (2) Personal representative acting for a decedent;
  (3) Conservatorship; and
  (4) Trustee.
(b) This chapter shall apply to a custodian if the user resides in this state or resided in this state at the time of the user's death.
(c) This chapter shall not apply to a digital asset of an employer used by an employee in the ordinary course of the employer's business.

## History

Added by 2018 Ga. Laws 560,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2018.
