# § 53-9-7. Distribution of assets

(1) Security is given;
(2) The court orders the personal representative to deliver the assets to the person; or
(3) The two-year period has expired, whichever occurs first, and in the meantime shall invest the assets in any investment that is authorized by law for personal representatives or that is authorized in the duly probated will of the missing individual and shall pay the income from such investments to the person or persons entitled to such assets quarterly or as otherwise directed by the court.
