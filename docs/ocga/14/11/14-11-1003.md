# § 14-11-1003. Notice of dissenters' rights

(a) If proposed limited liability company action creating dissenters' rights under Code Section 14-11-1002 is submitted to a vote at a members' meeting, the meeting notice must state that members are or may be entitled to assert dissenters' rights under this article and be accompanied by a copy of this article.
(b) If limited liability company action creating dissenters' rights under Code Section 14-11-1002 is taken without a vote of members, the limited liability company shall notify in writing all members entitled to assert dissenters' rights that the action was taken and send them the dissenters' notice described in Code Section 14-11-1005.
