# § 14-9A-116. Affidavits of capital paid in

At the time of filing the original certificate required under Code Section 14-9A-113, with the evidence of the acknowledgment thereof, an affidavit or affidavits of the several general partners shall also be filed in the same office, stating that the sums specified in the certificate to have been contributed by each of the special partners to the common stock have been actually and in good faith paid in cash.
