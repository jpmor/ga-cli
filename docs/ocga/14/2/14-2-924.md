# § 14-2-924. Annual meeting

(a) The annual meeting date for a statutory close corporation is the first business day after the thirty-first day of May unless its articles of incorporation, bylaws, or a shareholder agreement authorized by Code Section 14-2-920 fixes a different date.
(b) A statutory close corporation need not hold an annual meeting unless one or more shareholders deliver written notice to the corporation requesting a meeting at least 30 days before the meeting date determined under subsection (a) of this Code section.
