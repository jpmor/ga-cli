# § 14-3-741. Standing

(a) A derivative proceeding may be brought by:
  (1) Any director;
  (2) Any member or members having 5 percent or more of the voting power; or
  (3) Fifty or more members, regardless of voting power.
(b) A member may not commence or maintain a derivative proceeding unless the member:
  (1) Was a member of the corporation at the time of the act or omission complained of or became a member through transfer by operation of law from one who was a member at that time; and
  (2) Fairly and adequately represents the interests of the corporation in enforcing the right of the corporation.
