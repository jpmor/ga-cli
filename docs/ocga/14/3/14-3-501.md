# § 14-3-501. Registered office and registered agent

(1) A registered office with the same address as that of the registered agent; and
(2) A registered agent, who may be:
  (A) A person who resides in this state and whose office is identical with the registered office;
  (B) A domestic corporation, business corporation, or domestic limited liability company formed under this title whose office is identical with the registered office; or
  (C) A foreign corporation, foreign business corporation, or foreign limited liability company authorized to transact business in this state whose office is identical with the registered office.

## History

Amended by 2023 Ga. Laws 260,§ 1-1, eff. 7/1/2023.
Amended by 2016 Ga. Laws 364,§ 3-3, eff. 7/1/2016.
