# § 14-3-1301. Dividends and distributions prohibited

Except as provided in Code Section 14-3-1302 and Article 14 of this chapter, a corporation shall not pay dividends to or make distributions of any part of its assets, income, or profits to its members, directors, or officers.

## History

Amended by 2023 Ga. Laws 260,§ 1-1, eff. 7/1/2023.
