# § 31-2-12. [Reserved] Pilot program to provide coverage for bariatric surgical procedures for treatment of obesity and related conditions; definitions; eligibility; requirements; evaluation report on two-year pilot program



## History

Amended by 2019 Ga. Laws 321,§ 31, eff. 5/12/2019.
Added by 2014 Ga. Laws 499,§ 1, eff. effective only if funds are specifically appropriated for the purposes of this Act in an Appropriations Act enacted by the General Assembly. If funds are so appropriated, then this Act shall become effective on the later of the date on which such Appropriations Act becomes effective or the beginning date of the fiscal year for which such appropriations are made.
Renumbered as § 31-2A-11 by 2011 Ga. Laws 244,§ 3-2, eff. 7/1/2011.
