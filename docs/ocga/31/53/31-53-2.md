# § 31-53-2. Definitions

(1) "Director" means the director of health strategy and coordination established pursuant to Code Section 31-53-4.
(2) "Office" means the Office of Health Strategy and Coordination established pursuant to Code Section 31-53-3.

## History

Added by 2019 Ga. Laws 41,§ 2-2, eff. 7/1/2019.
