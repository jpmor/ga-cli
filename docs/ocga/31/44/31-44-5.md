# § 31-44-5. Exceptions to licensing requirement

(1) A hospital permitted under Chapter 7 of this title that provides dialysis to individuals receiving services from the hospital;
(2) The office of a physician unless the office is used primarily as an end stage renal disease facility; or
(3) Federal or state agency facilities.

## History

Amended by 2005 Ga. Laws 371,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2005.
