# § 31-40-6. Enforcement of chapter; inspection of premises

The department and the county boards of health and their duly authorized agents are authorized and empowered to enforce compliance with this chapter and the rules and regulations adopted and promulgated under this chapter and, in connection therewith, to enter upon and inspect the premises of a body art studio at any reasonable time and in a reasonable manner, as provided in Article 2 of Chapter 5 of this title.
