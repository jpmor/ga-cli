# § 31-22-6. Power of board to promulgate rules and regulations

In addition to powers conferred elsewhere in this chapter, the board shall promulgate rules and regulations for the implementation of this chapter.

## History

Amended by 2021 Ga. Laws 223,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2021.
Amended by 2012 Ga. Laws 749,§ 3, eff. 7/1/2012.
