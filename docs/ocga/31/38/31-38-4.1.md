# § 31-38-4.1. Regulation of tanning facilities

(a) After January 1, 2011, no person shall establish, maintain, or operate a tanning facility without first having registered with the department.
(b) A person shall register under this Code section by submitting a form to the department. The form shall require only the name, address, and telephone number of the tanning facility and owner and the model number and type of each ultraviolet lamp used in the tanning facility.
(c) A registrant shall be required to pay an annual registration fee of $25.00 per tanning facility and an additional registration fee of $15.00 per tanning device owned, leased, or otherwise used by the tanning facility.

## History

Added by 2010 Ga. Laws 491,§ 2-1, eff. 7/1/2010.
