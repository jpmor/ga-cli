# § 31-49-4. Distribution of information

(a) The department shall post the information developed by the council pursuant to paragraph (2) of subsection (a) of Code Section 31-49-3 on its website.
(b) Subject to appropriations or access to other private or public funds, the department may distribute such information to individuals with lupus, their family members, health care professionals, hospitals, local health departments, schools, agencies on aging, employers, health plans, women's health groups, and nonprofit and community based organizations.

## History

Added by 2014 Ga. Laws 564,§ 2, eff. 7/1/2014.
