# § 31-7-434. Informational materials; public accessibility

Each hospital and long-term care facility shall post on its website informational materials developed by the Department of Community Health explaining the rights specified in this article. The Department of Community Health shall develop such informational materials and shall make such materials available to hospitals and long-term care facilities for the purposes of this Code section.

## History

Added by 2024 Ga. Laws 573,§ 2, eff. 7/1/2024.
