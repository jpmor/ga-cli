# § 47-12-1. Definitions

(1) "Board of trustees" means the Board of Trustees of the District Attorneys Retirement Fund of Georgia.
(2) "Fund" means the District Attorneys Retirement Fund of Georgia.
(3) "Member" means a member of the District Attorneys Retirement Fund of Georgia.
(4) "Salary," "fees," and "compensation" includes all emoluments received by a district attorney for his services or resulting from the performance of his duties.
