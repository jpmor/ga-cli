# § 47-12-21. Creation of the fund; membership of the board of trustees; payments to the fund

(a) There is created the District Attorneys Retirement Fund of Georgia.
(b) The board of trustees of the fund shall be the Governor, the state treasurer, and an appointee of the Governor who is not the Attorney General. All payments to this fund shall be made to the board of trustees.

## History

Amended by 2010 Ga. Laws 621,§ 3, eff. 7/1/2010.
