# § 47-17-62. Payment of employment contributions by Board of Dentistry

The Georgia Board of Dentistry shall pay an employer contribution for each of its investigators who becomes a member of the fund . Such contribution shall be the full actuarial cost of the member's participation as calculated by the actuary for the fund and shall be made on an annual basis.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 562,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2024.
Added by 2018 Ga. Laws 311,§ 2, eff. 7/1/2018.
