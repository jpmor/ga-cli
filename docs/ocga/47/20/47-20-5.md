# § 47-20-5. Common law duties of trustees applicable

The duties of the boards of trustees of public retirement systems or pension plans contained in this title are in addition to, and not in limitation of, the common law duties of the trustee found in Title 53 except to the extent inconsistent with those within this title.

## History

Added by 2013 Ga. Laws 216,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2013.
