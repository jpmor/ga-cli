# § 47-16-43. Requirements for continued active membership in the fund

(1) Continue serving as a sheriff;
(2) Pay to the secretary-treasurer membership dues of $65.00 per month. Such payment shall be due on or before the tenth day of the following month until the member has made such payments for a total of 30 years; and
(3) Comply with all other mandatory provisions of this chapter and all rules and regulations promulgated by the board.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 569,§ 2, eff. 7/1/2024.
