# § 47-11-1. Definitions

(1) "Adjusted annual income" means the maximum income determined by application of paragraph (5) of Code Section 47-11-40.
(2) "Adjusted monthly income" means a sum equal to one-twelfth of the adjusted annual income.
(3) "Board" means the Board of Commissioners of the Judges of the Probate Courts Retirement Fund of Georgia.
(4) "Fund" means the Judges of the Probate Courts Retirement Fund of Georgia.
(5) "Member" means a member of the Judges of the Probate Courts Retirement Fund of Georgia.
