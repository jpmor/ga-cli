# § 47-8-2. Creation of the Superior Court Judges Retirement Fund of Georgia; trustees of the fund; payment to the fund

There is created the Superior Court Judges Retirement Fund of Georgia. The trustees of the fund shall be the Governor, the state treasurer, and an appointee of the Governor who is not the Attorney General. All payments to this fund shall be made to the board of trustees.

## History

Amended by 2010 Ga. Laws 621,§ 3, eff. 7/1/2010.
