# § 7-1-1115. Maintenance of federal branch or agency

(a) A foreign bank licensed under this article to establish and maintain a Georgia state branch or Georgia state agency shall not concurrently maintain a federal branch or federal agency in this state.
(b) A foreign bank which maintains a federal branch or federal agency in this state shall not concurrently be licensed under this article to maintain a Georgia state branch or Georgia state agency.

## History

Added by 2023 Ga. Laws 348,§ 48, eff. 7/1/2023.
