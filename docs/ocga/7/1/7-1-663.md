# § 7-1-663. Rules and regulations

Without limitation on the authority conferred by Article 1 of this chapter, the department is authorized to make such rules and regulations not inconsistent with this article and other applicable statutes governing the operation of credit unions as it may consider reasonable and proper for the protection of all funds invested. The department shall solicit comments from credit unions at least annually for recommended changes to the department's rules and regulations.

## History

Amended by 2005 Ga. Laws 160,§ 21, eff. 5/5/2005.
