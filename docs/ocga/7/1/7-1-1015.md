# § 7-1-1015. Rules relative to escrow accounts

The department may promulgate rules with respect to the placement in escrow accounts by any person required to be licensed by this article of any money, fund, deposit, check, or draft entrusted to it by any persons dealing with it as a residential mortgage loan originator, mortgage broker, mortgage lender, or servicer.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 474,§ 1-32, eff. 7/1/2024.
Amended by 2009 Ga. Laws 66,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2009.
