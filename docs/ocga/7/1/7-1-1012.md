# § 7-1-1012. Rules and regulations

Without limitation on the power conferred by Article 1 of this chapter, the department may make reasonable rules and regulations, not inconsistent with law, for the enforcement of this article, to effectuate the purposes of this article, and to clarify the meaning of terms.

## History

Amended by 2009 Ga. Laws 66,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2009.
Amended by 2001 Ga. Laws 299, § 15, eff. 7/1/2001.
