# § 7-1-1138. Continuing registration

Every Georgia state representative office registered with the department under the provisions of Code Section 7-1-721 as of June 30, 2023, shall remain registered and be allowed to conduct business in Georgia. Such Georgia state representative offices shall comply with this part and Part 1 of this article.

## History

Added by 2023 Ga. Laws 348,§ 48, eff. 7/1/2023.
