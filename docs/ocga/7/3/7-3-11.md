# § 7-3-11. § 7-3-11. Failure to begin business or suspending activities after license issued

In the event a licensee does not begin the operation of business under such license within a period of 120 days from the date of the issuance of such license or in the event a licensee, after having begun the operation of business under the license, remains inactive in such business for a period of 120 days, such license shall be subject to suspension or revocation by the Commissioner after notice and hearing under the procedure provided in Code Section 7-3-24 for the revocation or suspension of licenses. Any order or decision of the Commissioner on such matter shall be subject to review as provided in Code Section 7-3-24.

Ga. L. 1964, p. 288, § 3; Ga. L. 1989, p. 14, § 7.
