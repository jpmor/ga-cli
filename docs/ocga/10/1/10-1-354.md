# § 10-1-354. Inspections by law enforcement officers

(1) Any and all regulated metal property in the possession of the secondary metals recycler; and
(2) Any and all records required to be maintained under Code Section 10-1-353.

## History

Renumbered from 10-1-352 and amended by 2012 Ga. Laws 590,§ I-1-1, eff. 7/1/2012.
