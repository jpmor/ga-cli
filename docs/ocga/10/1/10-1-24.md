# § 10-1-24. Timing of assignments of note or other evidence of indebtedness

The seller in a door-to-door sale shall not negotiate, transfer, sell, or assign any note or other evidence of indebtedness to a finance company or other third party prior to midnight on the second day following the end of the period during which the buyer has a right to cancel pursuant to this article.

## History

Added by 2023 Ga. Laws 263,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2023.
