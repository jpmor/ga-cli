# § 10-1-600. Maintenance of records; inspection

(a) Each buying service or club licensed in this state shall keep and maintain:
  (1) Accurate accounts, books, and records of all transactions in this state;
  (2) Copies of all agreements;
  (3) Dates and amounts of payments made and accepted thereon; and
  (4) The names and addresses of all members in this state.
(b) Such accounts, books, and records shall be open for inspection by the Attorney General during normal business hours on all normal business days.

## History

Amended by 2015 Ga. Laws 187,§ 6, eff. 7/1/2015.
