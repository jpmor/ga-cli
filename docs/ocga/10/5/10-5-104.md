# § 10-5-104. Limitations on administrative and civil liability

(1) A delay of a disbursement or transaction pursuant to this article;
(2) A disclosure made pursuant to this article; or
(3) The failure to notify an eligible adult of disclosures made pursuant to this article.

## History

Added by 2023 Ga. Laws 269,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2023.
