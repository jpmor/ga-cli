# § 10-5-20. Restrictions on sales of securities

(1) The security is a federal covered security;
(2) The security, transaction, or offer is exempted from registration under Code Sections 10-5-10 through 10-5-12; or
(3) The security is registered under this chapter.

## History

Added by 2008 Ga. Laws 528,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2009.
