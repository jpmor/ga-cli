# § 10-5-105. Disclosure on investigations

Notwithstanding any provision of law to the contrary, the Commissioner may disclose to any notifying broker-dealer, investment adviser, or qualified individual the general status or final disposition of any investigation that arose from a report made by the broker-dealer, investment adviser, or qualified individual under this article.

## History

Added by 2023 Ga. Laws 269,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2023.
