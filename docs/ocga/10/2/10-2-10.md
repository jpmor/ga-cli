# § 10-2-10. Delivery tickets for bulk sales and bulk deliveries of heating fuel

(1) The name and address of the vendor and purchaser;
(2) The date delivered;
(3) The quantity delivered and the quantity upon which the price is based, if this differs from the delivered quantity;
(4) The identity of the goods or commodities in the most descriptive terms commercially practicable, including any quantity representation made in connection with the sale; and
(5) The count of individually wrapped packages, if more than one.

## History

Amended by 2017 Ga. Laws 275,§ 10, eff. 5/9/2017.
