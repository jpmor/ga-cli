# § 13-8-38. Agreements to which article shall apply

The provisions of this article shall apply to all written or oral agreements between a manufacturer with a wholesaler including, but not limited to, the franchise offering, the franchise agreement, sales of goods, services and advertising, leases or mortgages of real or personal property, promises to pay, security interests, pledges, insurance contracts, advertising contracts, construction or installation contracts, servicing contracts, and all other such agreements in which the manufacturer has any direct or indirect interest.
