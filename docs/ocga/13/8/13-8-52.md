# § 13-8-52. Application

(a) The provisions of this article shall be applicable only to contracts and agreements between or among:
  (1) Employers and employees;
  (2) Distributors and manufacturers;
  (3) Lessors and lessees;
  (4) Partnerships and partners;
  (5) Franchisors and franchisees;
  (6) Sellers and purchasers of a business or commercial enterprise; and
  (7) Two or more employers.
(b) The provisions of this article shall not apply to any contract or agreement not described in subsection (a) of this Code section.

## History

Added by 2011 Ga. Laws 99,§ 4, eff. 5/11/2011.
