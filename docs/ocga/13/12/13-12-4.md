# § 13-12-4. Applicability

(1) A financial institution as provided in Chapter 1 or 2 of Title 7 or any depository institution as defined in 12 U.S.C. Section 1813(c)(2);
(2) A foreign bank maintaining a branch or agency licensed under the laws of any state of the United States;
(3) Any subsidiary or affiliate of an entity provided in paragraph (1) or (2) of this Code section;
(4) Any electric utility as provided in Chapter 3 of Title 46;
(5) Any entity regulated pursuant to Chapter 45 of Title 43; or
(6) Any county, municipal corporation, authority, or local government or governing body.

## History

Added by 2013 Ga. Laws 330,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2013.
