# § 45-25-4. Methods of funding

The governing authority of any county, municipality, or consolidated government is authorized to use available revenues, including, but not limited to, proceeds from county and municipal taxes imposed under Chapter 8 of Title 33, for purposes of providing insurance under this chapter.

## History

Added by 2024 Ga. Laws 511,§ 2, eff. 1/1/2025.
