# § 45-19-32. Unlawful practice for party to violate conciliation agreement

It is an unlawful practice for a party to a conciliation agreement made pursuant to subsection (f) of Code Section 45-19-36 to violate the terms of the agreement.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 395,§ 4, eff. 7/1/2024.
