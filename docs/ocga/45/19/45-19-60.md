# § 45-19-60. Purpose of article

It shall be the policy of this state to be a model employer of individuals with disabilities by encouraging state agencies to consider the best practices relating to the recruitment, hiring, advancement, and retention of a broad range of qualified individuals with disabilities at all levels and for all occupations.

## History

Added by 2024 Ga. Laws 558,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2024.
