# § 45-12-154. Reporting to General Assembly

(1) A description of the implementation of the GDAC Project, including identification of the sources and types of data received and maintained by the GDAC Project over the prior 12 months;
(2) A list of all aggregated data maintained by the GDAC Project;
(3) A description of each IRB approved disclosure of data or data sets by the GDAC Project;
(4) A list of publications and other reports based on GDAC Project data;
(5) A strategic plan for achieving the purposes of this part during the successive 12 month period; and
(6) Any other information deemed appropriate by the office.

## History

Added by 2019 Ga. Laws 154,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2019.
