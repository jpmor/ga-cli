# § 45-12-177. Office to review and establish state goals and policies; Governor to prepare annual policy document reflecting state strategic plan

(a) The Office of Planning and Budget shall have the power and duty to review and consider immediate and long-range state agency proposals, goals, and directions and to establish state-wide goals and policies.
(b) The Governor, through the Office of Planning and Budget, shall prepare an annual policy document to reflect the state strategic plan and address state-wide goals, objectives, and opportunities. A program budget report shall satisfy this requirement. Such policy document shall be transmitted to the General Assembly at the beginning of each legislative session beginning with the 2006 session.

## History

Amended by 2005 Ga. Laws 322,§ 7, eff. 5/9/2005.
