# § 45-3-13. Loyalty oath - Form

The oath prescribed in Code Section 45-3-11 shall be in the following form:

"I, __________________ (Name) a citizen of __________________ and being an employee of __________________ and the recipient of public funds for services rendered as such employee, do hereby solemnly swear and affirm that I will support the Constitution of the United States and the Constitution of Georgia."

## History

Amended by 2015 Ga. Laws 70,§ 5-4, eff. 7/1/2015.
