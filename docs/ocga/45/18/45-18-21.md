# § 45-18-21. Deposit of employer and retiree contributions into the Georgia Retiree Health Benefit Fund

Notwithstanding any other provisions of this part, the board shall deposit into the Georgia Retiree Health Benefit Fund created by Code Section 45-18-101 the individual contributions by retirees and the employer contributions respecting retirees provided for by this part.
