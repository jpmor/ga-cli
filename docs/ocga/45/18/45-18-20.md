# § 45-18-20. Fiscal note required for bills impacting employees' health insurance plans

Any bill prepared by the General Assembly which would impact the state employees' health insurance plan established under this part shall require a fiscal note in accordance with the procedures of Code Section 28-5-42.

## History

Amended by 2010 Ga. Laws 624,§ 45, eff. 6/3/2010.
Added by 2006 Ga. Laws 506,§ 12, eff. 7/1/2006.
