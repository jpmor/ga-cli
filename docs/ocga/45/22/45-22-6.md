# § 45-22-6. Rules and regulations; consultation

(a) The department shall promulgate such rules and regulations as may be necessary to administer this chapter.
(b) The department shall consult with persons knowledgeable in the field of hazardous chemicals to assist the department in carrying out its duties under this chapter.

## History

Amended by 2015 Ga. Laws 137,§ 3, eff. 1/1/2016.
