# § 45-10-27. Construction of part with provisions, rules, and regulations of Chapter 20 of this title

This part shall in no way supersede any provision of Chapter 20 of this title or any rule or regulation promulgated pursuant thereto.

## History

Amended by 2012 Ga. Laws 630,§ 2-72, eff. 7/1/2012.
Amended by 2009 Ga. Laws 155,§ 2, eff. 5/5/2009.
