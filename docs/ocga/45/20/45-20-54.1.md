# § 45-20-54.1. Promulgation of regulations regarding distribution of deducted funds; disposition of undesignated funds

The board shall promulgate regulations necessary and expedient to accomplishing the distribution of funds deducted from employees' salaries, honoring employee designations. Undesignated funds shall be fairly and impartially distributed as determined by the board.

## History

Amended by 2012 Ga. Laws 630,§ 1-1, eff. 7/1/2012.
