# § 45-20-18. Loss of eligibility for wage incentive payment due to abuse of member of public

Any state employee who commits a validated act of abuse towards a member of the public while performing employment duties shall not be eligible for any wage incentive payment during the period such act occurred.

## History

Amended by 2012 Ga. Laws 630,§ 1-1, eff. 7/1/2012.
