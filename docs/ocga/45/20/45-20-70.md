# § 45-20-70. "Employee assistance program" defined

As used in this article, the term "employee assistance program" or "program" means a service established to assist state employees in coping with and overcoming persistent problems that jeopardize the employee's effective job performance.

## History

Amended by 2012 Ga. Laws 630,§ 1-1, eff. 7/1/2012.
