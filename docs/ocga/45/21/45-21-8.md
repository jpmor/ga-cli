# § 45-21-8. Costs to be borne by board and appointing authorities

The board shall bear the costs of administration and of the certificates and emblems which it awards and appointing authorities shall bear the costs of administration and of the certificates, emblems, and meritorious awards and payments which they award.

## History

Amended by 2006 Ga. Laws 753,§ 1, eff. 5/3/2006.
Amended by 2001 Ga. Laws 213, § 1, eff. 4/26/2001.
