# § 21-2-329. Delivery of voting machine keys to chief manager

(1) The number of the voting machine;
(2) The designation of the precinct;
(3) The number of the seal; and
(4) The number registered on the protective counter or device as reported by the custodian; provided, however, that, if the type of voting machine used requires the simultaneous use of three keys to unlock the registering counters or counter compartment, only two of the said keys shall be enclosed in such sealed envelope, the third key being retained by the custodian or the superintendent.
