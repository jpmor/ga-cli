# § 21-2-36. Notification of proposed consent agreement, settlement, or consent order

The State Election Board, the members thereof, the Secretary of State, and any of their attorneys or staff, at least five business days prior to entering into any consent agreement, settlement, or consent order that limits, alters, or interprets any provision of this chapter, shall notify the House of Representatives and Senate Committees on the Judiciary of such proposed consent agreement, settlement, or consent order.

## History

Added by 2021 Ga. Laws 9,§ 8, eff. 3/25/2021.
