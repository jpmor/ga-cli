# § 21-2-411. Return of checked list of electors and voter's certificates to superintendent; disposition of list and certificates by registrars

The chief manager in each precinct shall return a checked list of electors, reflecting those who voted, and the voter's certificates to the superintendent, to be deposited with the registrars. The board of registrars shall keep such voter's certificates for at least 24 months and such electors lists for at least five years.
