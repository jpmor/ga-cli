# § 21-2-105. Local election official defined

(1) A county board of elections or a county board of elections and registration established pursuant to Code Section 21-2-40or 21-2-40.1; or
(2) A municipal election superintendent.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 580,§ 10, eff. 1/1/2025.
Added by 2021 Ga. Laws 9,§ 12, eff. 3/25/2021.
