# § 21-2-268. Compensation for rent, heat, light, and janitorial services

The superintendent or county or municipal governing authority shall fix the compensation for rent, heat, light, and janitorial services to be paid for the use of polling places for primaries and elections; provided, however, that no compensation for rent, heat, or light shall be paid in the case of schoolhouses, municipal buildings or rooms, or other public buildings used as polling places.

## History

Amended by 2005 Ga. Laws 53,§ 32, eff. 7/1/2005.
