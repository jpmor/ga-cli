# § 21-5-74. Postemployment restrictions on lobbyists

A lobbyist shall not be eligible for executive appointment to any board, authority, commission, or bureau created and established by the laws of this state which regulates the activities of a business, firm, corporation, or agency that the lobbyist represented until one year after the expiration of the lobbyist's registration for that business, firm, corporation, or agency.

## History

Added by 2005 Ga. Laws 212,§ 23, eff. 1/9/2006.
