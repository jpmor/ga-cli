# § 26-2-440. Definitions

(1) "Administration" means the United States Food and Drug Administration.
(2) "Department" means the Georgia Department of Agriculture.
(3) "Federal act" means the Federal Food Safety Modernization Act, 21 U.S.C. Section 301, et seq.

## History
