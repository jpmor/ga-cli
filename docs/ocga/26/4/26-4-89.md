# § 26-4-89. Selling or dispensing prescription drugs by vending machine

(a) Any person who shall sell or dispense prescription drugs by the use of vending machines shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.
(b) A remote automated medication system shall not be considered a vending machine for purposes of this Code section.

## History
