# § 44-14-613. Effect of article on other laws

(a) This article shall in no way affect or alter the laws of this state with respect to personal property.
(b) This article shall in no way affect or alter the laws of this state with respect to judgments, executions, and attachments; the liens they create; their enforceability; the recording of executions in general execution dockets; the notice given by the recording; or otherwise.
