# § 44-5-12. Impeachment of grants; grounds; effect of irregularities or misnomer

(1) Void upon their face;
(2) Issued without authority of law or against a prohibition in a law; or
(3) Issued for property to which the state had no title. However, mere irregularities in the proceedings to obtain grants shall not be inquired into nor may a mistake in the name of the grantee be proved by parol.
