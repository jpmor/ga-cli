# § 44-3-177. Exemption from other state laws requiring registration and public offering statements

(a) Any time-share program registered under this article in which a public offering statement has been prepared shall not require registration under any of the following:
  (1) Article 1 of this chapter;
  (2) Chapter 5 of Title 10; or
  (3) Any other state law which requires the preparation of a public offering statement or substantially similar document for distribution to purchasers.
(b) Any time-share program registered under this article that fails to restrict the price at which an owner may sell or exchange such owner's time-share interval shall not by virtue of such failure cause the time-share interval to become a security under Chapter 5 of Title 10; nor shall an exchange program offering such a time-share interval for exchange be construed to be offering a security under Chapter 5 of Title 10.
