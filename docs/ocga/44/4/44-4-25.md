# § 44-4-25. Use of terms limited

The use of the term "Georgia State Plane Coordinate System East Zone," "Georgia State Plane Coordinate System West Zone," on any map, report of survey, or other document shall be limited to coordinates based on the Georgia State Plane Coordinate Systems as defined in this article.

## History

Renumbered from § 44-4-26 and amended by 2022 Ga. Laws 850,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2022.
