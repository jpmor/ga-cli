# § 44-4-28. Use of system not mandatory

Nothing contained in this article shall require any purchaser or mortgagee to rely on a description, any part of which depends exclusively upon the Georgia State Plane Coordinate System. Nothing in this article shall be so construed as to require any person, firm, or corporation to use this system of coordinates to obtain or secure a legal description of land or real estate.

## History

Renumbered from § 44-4-29 and amended by 2022 Ga. Laws 850,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2022.
