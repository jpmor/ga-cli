# § 44-4-23. Description of land extending from one zone to another

When any tract of land to be defined by a single description extends from one into the other of the coordinate zones established in Code Section 44-4-21, the positions of all points on its boundaries may be referred to either of the two zones, the zone which is used being specifically named in the description.

## History

Amended by 2022 Ga. Laws 850,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2022.
