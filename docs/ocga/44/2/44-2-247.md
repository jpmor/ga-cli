# § 44-2-247. Notation of judgment

The regular form to be used for the notation of a judgment on the title register is as follows:

NOTATION OF JUDGMENT

To the clerk of the Superior Court, __________________ County, Georgia:

Please note on certificate of title No. __________________, Registered Title No. __________________, a judgment issued from

__________________

Court of __________________ in favor

of __________________ v. __________________

for the amount of $__________________.

This __________________ day of __________________, __________________.

This __________________ day of __________________, __________________.

__________________
