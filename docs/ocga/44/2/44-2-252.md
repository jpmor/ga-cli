# § 44-2-252. Updating entries and notations on owner's certificate; clerk's endorsement

The holder of an uncanceled owner's certificate of title may at any time present it to the clerk and have the clerk enter on the owner's certificate all entries and notations of every kind which appear on the certificate of title which have not already been entered on the owner's certificate. The clerk shall thereupon endorse on the owner's certificate the words "Valid, with all entries noted to this date. This __________________ day of __________________, __________________, at __________________: __________________ __________________. M." and shall officially sign the endorsement.
