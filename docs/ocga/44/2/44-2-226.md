# § 44-2-226. Certificate of mailing; entry of such certificate on petition

The clerk should also enter on the petition a certificate substantially in the following form:

I certify that on the __________________ day of __________________, __________________, I mailed to each of the following persons a copy of the within petition and process to his post office address as disclosed by the record:

__________________

__________________

__________________

and that advertisement has been published in accordance with law, a copy of said advertisement being hereto attached.

This __________________ day of __________________, __________________.

__________________ Clerk
