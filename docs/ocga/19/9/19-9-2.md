# § 19-9-2. Right of surviving parent to custody of child; discretion of judge

(1) May exercise discretion as to the custody of the child, looking solely to the child's best interest and welfare; and
(2) In the case of a surviving parent being criminally indicted for the offense of murder or voluntary manslaughter of the other parent, can proceed in the court's discretion.

## History

Amended by 2007 Ga. Laws 264,§ 5, eff. 1/1/2008.
