# § 2-3-1. Short title

This chapter shall be known and may be cited as the "Georgia Agricultural Exposition Authority Act."

## History

Renumbered from § 12-3-470 by 2011 Ga. Laws 66,§ 4, eff. 7/1/2011.
Amended by 2011 Ga. Laws 66,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2011.
Former §§ 2-3-1 through 2-3-12 renumbered as § 12-3-650 by 2001 Ga. Laws 288, § 1, eff. 7/1/2001.
