# § 2-22-3. Provision of statistical information and data to determine compensation

Any integrator or processor shall provide to any contract grower or contract producer upon request thereby any statistical information and data used to determine compensation paid to such contract grower or contract producer under a production contract, other than a trade secret as defined by Code Section 10-1-761.

## History

Added by 2004 Ga. Laws 568, § 1, eff. 7/1/2004.
