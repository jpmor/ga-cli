# § 2-8-134. Limited application of marketing orders

Marketing orders issued by the commission may be limited in their application by prescribing the marketing areas or portions of the state in which a particular order shall be effective, provided that no marketing order shall be issued by the commission unless it embraces all persons of a like class.

## History

Added by 2006 Ga. Laws 652,§ 2, eff. 1/1/2007.
