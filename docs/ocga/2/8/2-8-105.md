# § 2-8-105. Rules and regulations

The commission is empowered to make all necessary rules and regulations for the purpose of carrying out the purposes of this article. The promulgation, adoption, and amendment of rules and regulations by the commission shall be subject to the requirements of Chapter 13 of Title 50, the "Georgia Administrative Procedure Act."

## History

Added by 2013 Ga. Laws 21,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2013.
