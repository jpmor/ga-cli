# § 2-8-213. Referendum vote on continuation of commission

A referendum shall be held once every five years to vote on the continuation of the commission. For the results to be valid, at least 50 percent of the eligible dealers in this state must vote, and if more than 50 percent of the dealers voting vote in favor of continuing the commission, then the commission will continue for another five years.

## History

Added by 2019 Ga. Laws 35,§ 1, eff. 4/18/2019.
