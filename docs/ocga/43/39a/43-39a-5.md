# § 43-39A-5. Status of appraiser classification issued by board to employee of commission

Any appraiser classification issued to an employee of the commission by the board shall be on inactive status during the time of the employee's employment with the commission. Any such employee shall not be required to pay a fee to keep an appraiser classification on an inactive status. Such appraiser classification shall be taken off inactive status and returned to the employee when that person's employment ends.
