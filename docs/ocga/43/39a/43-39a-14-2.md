# § 43-39A-14-2. Composition of appraisal panel

An appraisal panel may include appraisers who are engaged by or accepted by the appraisal management company for consideration for future appraisal assignments in covered transactions or for secondary mortgage market participants in connection with covered transactions.

## History

Added by 2019 Ga. Laws 241,§ 7, eff. 7/1/2019.
