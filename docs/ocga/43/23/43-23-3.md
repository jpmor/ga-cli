# § 43-23-3. Seal

The board shall adopt a seal, which may be either an engraved or an ink stamped seal, with the words "Board of Landscape Architects, State of Georgia" or such other device as the board may desire included thereon, by which it shall authenticate the acts of the board.

## History

Amended by 2011 Ga. Laws 52,§; 69, eff. 1/1/2013.
