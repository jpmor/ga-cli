# § 43-33-7. Conduct of business by telephone or other digital means

With the exception of hearings in contested cases, the board may conduct business in conference by telephone or other digital means, provided that members of the board shall not receive compensation for business conducted in conference by telephone or other digital means.

## History

Amended by 2015 Ga. Laws 53,§ 2, eff. 7/1/2015.
