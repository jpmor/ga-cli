# § 43-46A-7. Good faith estimate of costs

Each trauma scene waste management practitioner registered under this chapter, prior to beginning the cleanup, removal, or remediation of trauma scene waste, shall provide the individual who requested such services with a good faith estimate of the expected costs of such services.

## History

Added by 2020 Ga. Laws 566,§ 1, eff. 1/1/2021.
