# § 43-46A-6. Recovery of costs

On and after January 1, 2021, it shall be against public policy for any person who is not properly registered under this chapter to seek to recover from the owner of any property or any other person the cost of the cleanup, removal, or remediation of trauma scene waste at, in, or on such property.

## History

Added by 2020 Ga. Laws 566,§ 1, eff. 1/1/2021.
