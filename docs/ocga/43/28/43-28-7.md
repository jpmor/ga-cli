# § 43-28-7. License requirement for occupational therapists

(1) Practice occupational therapy or assist in the practice of occupational therapy; or
(2) Hold himself or herself out as an occupational therapist or an occupational therapy assistant or as being able to render occupational therapy services in this state.

## History

Amended by 2019 Ga. Laws 246,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2019.
