# § 43-34-180. Advisory committee

The board shall appoint an advisory committee. The advisory committee shall include clinical perfusionists licensed under this article and such members as the board in its discretion may determine. Members shall receive no compensation for service on the committee. The committee shall have such advisory duties and responsibilities as the board may determine. Advisory committee members must be licensed pursuant to this article.

## History

Amended by 2009 Ga. Laws 243,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2009.
Added by 2002 Ga. Laws 786, § 1, eff. 7/1/2002.
