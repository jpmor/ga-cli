# § 43-34-34. Limited provisional licenses

A person who held a valid provisional license on or before April 16, 1979, shall be able to renew such license annually without any one-time-only renewal limitation, as long as such person continues to meet the other requirements specified in this article and does not otherwise violate this article.

## History

Amended by 2009 Ga. Laws 243,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2009.
