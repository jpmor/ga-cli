# § 43-34-28. Reciprocity; expedited license

(a) The board may grant a license without examination to licensees of boards of other states requiring equal or higher qualifications.
(b) The board may grant an expedited license to licensees of boards of other states pursuant to the Interstate Medical Licensure Compact Act contained in Article 11 of this chapter.

## History

Amended by 2009 Ga. Laws 243,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2009.
