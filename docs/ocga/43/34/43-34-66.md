# § 43-34-66. License surrender upon demand; display requirements; change of address

(a) Any document evidencing licensure issued by the board is the property of the board and must be surrendered on demand.
(b) Every person who holds a license issued by the board in accordance with this article and who is engaged in the active practice of acupuncture or the active practice of auricular (ear) detoxification therapy as an auricular (ear) detoxification technician shall display the document evidencing licensure in an appropriate and public manner.
(c) Every person who holds a license issued by the board shall inform the board of any change of address.

## History

Amended by 2009 Ga. Laws 243,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2009.
