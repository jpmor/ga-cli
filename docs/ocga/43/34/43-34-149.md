# § 43-34-149. Sanctions

The board, in consultation with the advisory committee, may impose on a respiratory care professional any sanction authorized under subsection (b) of Code Section 43-34-8 upon a finding of any conduct specified in subsection (a) of Code Section 43-34-8.

## History

Amended by 2009 Ga. Laws 243,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2009.
