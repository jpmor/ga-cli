# § 43-34-303. Voting representatives serving as commissioners on the Interstate Medical Licensure Compact Commission

The Georgia Composite Medical Board shall designate the two voting representatives from this state who shall serve as Commissioners on the Interstate Medical Licensure Compact Commission in accordance with Section 11(d) of the Interstate Medical Licensure Compact entered into pursuant to Code Section 43-34-301.

## History

Added by 2019 Ga. Laws 46,§ 4, eff. 7/1/2019.
