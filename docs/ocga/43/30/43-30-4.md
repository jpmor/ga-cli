# § 43-30-4. Election of board officers; rules, regulations, and bylaws as to board's proceedings; meetings

The board shall annually elect a president and a vice-president who shall hold their offices until their successors are elected and qualified. The board shall prescribe such rules, regulations, and bylaws for its proceedings and governance as will put this chapter into effect. There shall be at least two regular meetings of the board held every year. Special meetings may be held on the call of the president and two other members.
