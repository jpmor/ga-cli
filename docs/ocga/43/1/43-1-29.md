# § 43-1-29. Suspension of license for nonpayment of student loans prohibited

A professional licensing board shall not suspend the license of a person licensed by that board because he or she has been certified by any entity of the federal government for nonpayment or default or breach of a repayment or service obligation under any federal educational loan, loan repayment, or service conditional scholarship program.

## History
