# § 43-26-64. Statutes applicable to nurses practicing under multistate license

A registered professional nurse or licensed practical nurse practicing in this state under a multistate license issued by another party state shall be subject to all requirements and duties applicable to registered professional nurses or licensed practical nurses who are licensed pursuant to Article 1 or Article 2 of this chapter, respectively.

## History

Added by 2017 Ga. Laws 175,§ 3, eff. 7/1/2017.
