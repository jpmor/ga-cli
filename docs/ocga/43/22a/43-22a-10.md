# § 43-22A-10. Sanction

The Secretary, in consultation with the advisory group, may impose on a licensed lactation consultant any sanction authorized under subsection (c) of Code Section 43-22A-12 upon a finding of any conduct specified in subsection (a) of Code Section 43-22A-12.

## History

Added by 2016 Ga. Laws 429,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2016.
