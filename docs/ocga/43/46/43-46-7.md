# § 43-46-7. Penalty; violation of chapter as constituting an unfair or deceptive act

(a) Any person, firm, or corporation who violates this chapter shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.
(b) In addition to the criminal penalty provided in subsection (a) of this Code section, any violation of this chapter shall constitute an unfair or deceptive act under Part 2 of Article 15 of Chapter 1 of Title 10, the "Fair Business Practices Act of 1975."
