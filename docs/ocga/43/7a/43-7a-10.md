# § 43-7A-10. Term of licensure and renewal

A license shall be granted for a period of two years. Prior to expiration of a license, the license may be renewed upon submission of an application for renewal, including proof of continued certification by a certifying entity and payment of the renewal fee imposed by the board.

## History

Added by 2022 Ga. Laws 811,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2023.
