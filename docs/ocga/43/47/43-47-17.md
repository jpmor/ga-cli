# § 43-47-17. Consent to inspection as condition of licensure

Every person required to be licensed under this chapter shall, as a condition of licensure, be deemed to have granted authority and permission to the board, to either division, or to any peace officer to inspect any record or document and any motor vehicle or motor vehicle part or accessory at or on the premises of his or her principal place of business, or any additional place of business, at any reasonable time during the day or night during reasonable business hours.
