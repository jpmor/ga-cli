# § 43-47-8.1. Penalty

Any person who violates any provision of Code Section 40-2-39.1 shall be deemed to be a licensee for the purpose of imposing sanctions and penalties under this chapter and for the purpose of granting the board jurisdiction over such violator.

## History

Amended by 2007 Ga. Laws 124,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2007.
