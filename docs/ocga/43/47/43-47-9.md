# § 43-47-9. Contents of licenses; display of licenses; endorsement of change of business location on licenses

The licenses issued pursuant to this chapter shall specify the location of each place of business or branch or other location occupied or to be occupied by the licensee in conducting his or her business; and the license or supplemental license issued therefor shall be conspicuously displayed on each of such premises. In the event any such location is changed, the appropriate division shall endorse the change of location on the license without charge.
