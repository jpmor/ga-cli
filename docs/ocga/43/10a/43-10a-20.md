# § 43-10A-20. Penalty

Any person violating Code Section 43-10A-19 or Code Section 43-10A-7 shall be guilty of a misdemeanor and, upon conviction thereof, shall be punished by a fine of not less than $500.00 nor more than $1,000.00 for each offense or by imprisonment for up to 12 months, or both.

## History

Amended by 2022 Ga. Laws 813,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2022.
