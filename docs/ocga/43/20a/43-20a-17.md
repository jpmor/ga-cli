# § 43-20A-17. Suspension of license for nonpayment, default, or breach of repayment or service obligation under certain educational loan or scholarship programs prohibited

The Secretary of State shall not suspend a license issued pursuant to this chapter because of nonpayment or default or breach of a repayment or service obligation under any federal educational loan, loan repayment, or service conditional scholarship program.

## History

Amended by 2019 Ga. Laws 146,§ 1-15, eff. 7/1/2019.
Added by 2008 Ga. Laws 775,§ 15, eff. 7/1/2008.
