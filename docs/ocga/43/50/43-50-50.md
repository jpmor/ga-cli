# § 43-50-50. Purpose of part

It is the purpose of this part to encourage more effective utilization of the skills of licensed veterinarians by enabling them to delegate certain veterinary health care tasks to licensed veterinary technicians where such delegation is consistent with the animal patient's health and welfare.

## History

Amended by 2006 Ga. Laws 443,§ 3, eff. 3/30/2006.
Added by 2003 Ga. Laws 257, § 1-1, eff. 7/1/2003.
