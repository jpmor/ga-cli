# § 43-50-91. [See Note] Facilities accredited by American Zoo and Aquarium Association or equivalent agency exempt

This article shall not apply to any facility owned by the federal, state, or any local government, a public or private college or university, or a zoological park or aquarium that is accredited by the American Zoo and Aquarium Association or other substantially equivalent nationally recognized accrediting agency as determined by the board.

## History

Renumbered from § 43-50-71 by 2006 Ga. Laws 443,§ 4, eff. 3/30/2006.
Added by 2003 Ga. Laws 257, § 1-1, eff. only upon the effective date of an appropriation of funds for purposes of said part of this Act as expressed in a line item making specific reference to full funding of this Act in an appropriations Act enacted by the General Assembly.
