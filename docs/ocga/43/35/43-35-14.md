# § 43-35-14. Examination of applicants

The board may require applicants to be examined upon the subjects required for the practice of podiatric medicine. Any standardized examination which the board shall approve may be administered to all applicants in lieu of or in conjunction with any other examination which the board may require.

## History

Amended by 2010 Ga. Laws 417,§ 38, eff. 5/20/2010.
