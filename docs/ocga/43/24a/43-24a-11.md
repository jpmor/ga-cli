# § 43-24A-11. Licensing examinations

(a) Examinations shall be administered to qualified applicants for massage therapy licensure at least twice each calendar year.
(b) Applicants may obtain their examination scores in accordance with such rules and regulations as the board may establish.

## History

Amended by 2019 Ga. Laws 213,§ 1, eff. 5/6/2019.
Added by 2005 Ga. Laws 391,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2006.
