# § 16-6-13.2. Civil forfeiture of motor vehicle

(a) As used in this Code section, the term "motor vehicle" shall have the same meaning as set forth in Code Section 40-1-1.
(b) Any motor vehicle used by a person to facilitate a violation of Code Section 16-6-10, 16-6-11 when the offense involved the pimping of a person to perform an act of prostitution, or 16-6-12 is declared to be contraband and no person shall have a property right in it.
(c) Any property subject to forfeiture pursuant to subsection (b) of this Code section shall be forfeited in accordance with the procedures set forth in Chapter 16 of Title 9.
