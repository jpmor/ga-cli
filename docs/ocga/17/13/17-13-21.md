# § 17-13-21. Definitions

(1) "Executive authority" includes the Governor and any person performing the functions of governor in a state other than this state.
(2) "Governor" includes any person performing the functions of governor by authority of the laws of this state.
(3) "State," referring to a state other than this state, includes the District of Columbia and any other state or territory, organized or unorganized, of the United States of America.
