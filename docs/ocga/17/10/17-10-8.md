# § 17-10-8. Payment of fine in felony case; rebate or refund of fine not permitted upon revocation of probation

(a) In a felony case, when a statutory fine amount is not set by law, upon conviction, the court may impose a fine not to exceed $100,000.00.
(b) In any case when probation is revoked, the defendant shall not be entitled to any rebate or refund of any part of the fine paid.

## History

Amended by 2018 Ga. Laws 416,§ 2-7, eff. 7/1/2018.
