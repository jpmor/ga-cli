# § 29-11-14. Special jurisdiction; emergency appointment of guardian

Except as otherwise provided in Code Section 29-11-13, a court that has appointed a guardian or issued a conservatorship order consistent with this chapter shall have exclusive and continuing jurisdiction over the proceeding until it is terminated by the court or the appointment or order expires by its own terms.

## History

Added by 2016 Ga. Laws 486,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2016.
