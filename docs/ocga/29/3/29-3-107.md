# § 29-3-107. Hearing

(a) Upon the court's own motion or upon timely motion by the minor or by any interested person, the court shall hold a hearing to consider the petition for receipt and acceptance of the foreign conservator.
(b) If any interested person challenges the validity of the foreign conservator or the authority of the foreign court to appoint the conservator, the court may stay this proceeding while the petitioner is afforded the opportunity to have the foreign court hear the challenge and determine its merits.

## History

Added by 2004 Ga. Laws 460, § 1, eff. 7/1/2005.
