# § 29-5-47. Levy upon property; writs of execution

(a) When a judgment has been obtained against the conservator or the surety on the bond of a conservator, or both, a levy may be made upon any property of any defendant in fi. fa.
(b) The court shall be authorized to enter a judgment and to issue a writ of execution against the conservator and surety on the bond and shall be authorized to grant judgment and execution in favor of the surety against the conservator upon payment of the judgment by the surety.

## History

Added by 2004 Ga. Laws 460, § 1, eff. 7/1/2005.
