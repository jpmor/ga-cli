# § 29-5-44. Payment of bond premium

(a) A conservator who is required to give bond, and who has given as security on the bond one or more licensed commercial sureties, may pay any bond premium from the estate.
(b) When the guardian is required to give bond pursuant to Code Section 29-4-30, the conservator shall, upon the request of the guardian, pay any bond premium from the estate.

## History

Amended by 2019 Ga. Laws 233,§ 20, eff. 1/1/2020.
Added by 2004 Ga. Laws 460, § 1, eff. 7/1/2005.
