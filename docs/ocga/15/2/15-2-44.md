# § 15-2-44. How costs taxed; notice of appeal and transcript not recorded

(a) When judgment is pronounced in any case, the clerk shall tax the costs thereof, which shall be entered without charge on the minutes at the foot of the judgment. The clerk shall make no charge for attaching the seal to the remittitur, nor for any precept issued by him, nor for anything except services actually rendered.
(b) The clerk shall not record the notice of appeal, transcript, or record from the court below.
