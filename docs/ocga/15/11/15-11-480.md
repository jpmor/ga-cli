# § 15-11-480. When jeopardy attaches

(a) When a child enters a denial to a petition alleging his or her delinquency, jeopardy attaches when the first witness is sworn at the adjudication hearing.
(b) When a child enters an admission to a petition alleging his or her delinquency, jeopardy attaches when the court accepts the admission.

## History

Added by 2013 Ga. Laws 127,§ 1-1, eff. 1/1/2014.
