# § 15-10-101. Eligibility of constables

(a) Each constable taking office on or after July 1, 2023, shall:
  (1) Be at least 21 years of age;
  (2) Be a citizen of the United States;
  (3) Be a registered voter; and
  (4) Have obtained a state accredited high school diploma or general educational development (GED) diploma.
(b) Additional qualifications for the office of chief constable or constable or both may be imposed by local law.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 574,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2024.
