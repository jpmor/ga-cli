# § 15-5-110. [Effective 7/1/2025] Definitions

(1) "Personally identifiable information" means any phone number or address affirmatively identified by a protected person for inclusion in the data base established and maintained pursuant to Code Section 15-5-111.
(2) "Protected person" means any current or former:
  (A) Elected or appointed judge or justice of this state and his or her spouse;
  (B) Elected or appointed judge of any county or municipality of this state and his or her spouse; and
  (C) Appointed judge or justice of the United States and his or her spouse.
(3) "Public posting or display" means to communicate information or otherwise make information available to the general public.
(4) "Publicly available content" means any written or electronic document or record that provides information or that serves as a document or record maintained, controlled, or in the possession of a state or local government entity that may be obtained by any person from the internet or from such state or local government agency upon request whether free of charge or for a fee.
(5) "State or local government entity" means any:
  (A) Agency of the executive branch of this state; or
  (B) Any local governing authority of a county or municipality of this state.

## History

Added by 2024 Ga. Laws 584,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2025.
