# § 8-6-3. Division landfill records; filing notice of existence of landfill

(a) The division shall maintain records on each landfill operated now or in the future in this state, which records shall include an accurate legal description of the boundaries of the landfill and the dates of its operation.
(b) The division shall file notice of the existence of the landfill with the clerk of the superior court of the county or counties within which the landfill is located.
