# § 8-3-312. Disbursements for operating expenses

Funds may be disbursed from the trust fund to pay expenses of the commission, to pay any and all operating expenses, and to pay for professional, technical, and clerical services provided the commission by the Department of Community Affairs or by other outside sources.

## History

Added by 2024 Ga. Laws 413,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2024.
