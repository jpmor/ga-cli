# § 11-9-524. Delay by filing office or authority

(1) The delay is caused by interruption of communication or computer facilities, war, emergency conditions, failure of equipment, or other circumstances beyond control of the filing office or authority; and
(2) The filing office or authority exercises reasonable diligence under the circumstances.

## History

Added by 2001 Ga. Laws 191, § 1, eff. 7/1/2001.
