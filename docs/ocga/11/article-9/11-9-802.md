# § 11-9-802. Savings clause

(a)Pre-effective date transactions or liens. Except as otherwise provided in this part, this article, as in effect on July 1, 2013, applies to a transaction or lien within its scope, even if the transaction or lien was entered into or created before July 1, 2013.
(b)Pre-effective date proceedings. This article, as in effect on July 1, 2013, does not affect an action, case, or proceeding commenced before July 1, 2013.

## History

Added by 2013 Ga. Laws 223,§ 20, eff. 7/1/2013.
