# § 11-9-804. Security interest unperfected before effective date

(1) Without further action, on July 1, 2013, if the applicable requirements for perfection under this article, as in effect on July 1, 2013, are satisfied before or at that time; or
(2) When the applicable requirements for perfection are satisfied if the requirements are satisfied after that time.

## History

Added by 2013 Ga. Laws 223,§ 20, eff. 7/1/2013.
