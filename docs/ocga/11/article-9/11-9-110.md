# § 11-9-110. Security interests arising under Article 2 or 2A of this title

(1) The security interest is enforceable, even if paragraph (3) of subsection (b) of Code Section 11-9-203 has not been satisfied;
(2) Filing is not required to perfect the security interest;
(3) The rights of the secured party after default by the debtor are governed by Article 2 or 2A of this title; and
(4) The security interest has priority over a conflicting security interest created by the debtor.

## History

Added by 2001 Ga. Laws 191, § 1, eff. 7/1/2001.
