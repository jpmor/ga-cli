# § 11-2-202. Final expression; parol or extrinsic evidence

(a) By course of performance, course of dealing, or usage of trade (Code Section 11-1-303); and
(b) By evidence of consistent additional terms unless the court finds the record to have been intended also as a complete and exclusive statement of the terms of the agreement.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 600,§ 5-9, eff. 7/1/2024.
Amended by 2015 Ga. Laws 167,§ 3B-2, eff. 1/1/2016.
