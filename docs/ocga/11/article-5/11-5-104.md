# § 11-5-104. Formal requirements

A letter of credit, confirmation, advice, transfer, amendment, or cancellation may be issued in any form that is a signed record.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 600,§ 5-31, eff. 7/1/2024.
Added by 2002 Ga. Laws 874,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2002.
A former section with this number was repealed and reenacted by Ga. Laws 874, § 1.
