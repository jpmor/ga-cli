# § 11-3-301. Person entitled to enforce instrument

(i) the holder of the instrument;
(ii) a nonholder in possession of the instrument who has the rights of a holder; or
(iii) a person not in possession of the instrument who is entitled to enforce the instrument pursuant to Code Section 11-3-309 or subsection (d) of Code Section 11-3-418. A person may be a person entitled to enforce the instrument even though the person is not the owner of the instrument or is in wrongful possession of the instrument.
