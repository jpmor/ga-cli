# § 3-13-1. Definitions

(1) "Alcoholic beverages for consumption on premises" shall mean malt beverages, wine, or distilled spirits by the drink for consumption on the premises only.
(2) "Licensee" shall mean the developer, owner, or operator of the REAP or the developer, owner, or operator of or any certified project or facility located in a REAP to whom a state retail consumption dealer license is issued.
(3) "Regional Economic Assistance Project" or "REAP" shall have the same meaning as in Code Section 50-8-190 and shall specifically include any certified project or facility located in a REAP.

## History

Added by 2008 Ga. Laws 505,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2008.
