# Chapter 10 - SALE OR POSSESSION OF DISTILLED SPIRITS IN DRY COUNTIES AND MUNICIPALITIES (§§ 3-10-1 — 3-10-15)

- Section 3-10-1 - Scope of chapter
- Section 3-10-2 - Sale, exchange, or other possession of distilled spirits
- Section 3-10-3 - Keeping of distilled spirits in building not exclusively used for dwelling deemed prima-facie evidence of possession for sale or distribution
- Section 3-10-4 - Quantities of distilled spirits which may be lawfully possessed
- Section 3-10-5 - Allowing use of premises for unlawful sale, manufacture, or other disposition of distilled spirits
- Section 3-10-6 - Forfeiture of rights of lessee or tenant where unlawful act performed upon premises with lessee's or tenant's knowledge or permission
- Section 3-10-7 - Transportation or shipment of distilled spirits for sale or use in violation of title; exceptions for possession for personal use and transportation through counties or municipalities
- Section 3-10-8 - Common nuisances - Defined; institution of quo warranto proceedings against clubs or associations maintaining nuisances
- Section 3-10-9 - Common nuisances - Proceedings for abatement of nuisances
- Section 3-10-10 - Existence of property rights in distilled spirits or vessels kept or used in violation of chapter; contraband
- Section 3-10-11 - Contraband apparatus and appliances; existence of property rights therein; summary destruction of contraband; governing procedure for forfeiture of vehicles, boats, and vessels
- Section 3-10-12 - Raw materials or substances, fixtures, implements, or apparatus used or intended for use in unlawful distillation or manufacture of distilled spirits declared contraband; property rights in contraband; governing procedures for seizure and forfeiture
- Section 3-10-13 - Duties of district attorneys as to investigation and prosecution of violations of chapter; duties of sheriffs
- Section 3-10-14 - Evidence as to color, odor, appearance, and taste of beverage manufactured, sold, or disposed of by defendant; burden of proof when defendant claims beverage not a distilled spirit
- Section 3-10-15 - Penalty for violations of provisions of chapter
