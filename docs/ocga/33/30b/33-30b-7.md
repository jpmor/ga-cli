# § 33-30B-7. Interpretation; renewal of policy

(a) A stop-loss or specific excess and aggregate contract issued under this chapter shall not be construed or interpreted as an accident and sickness insurance policy.
(b) No stop-loss or specific excess and aggregate policy may be cancelled or nonrenewed because of the level of health care claims.

## History

Added by 2003 Ga. Laws 396, § 1, eff. 7/1/2003.
