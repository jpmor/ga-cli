# § 33-32-6. Tobacco crop insurance coverage

Any insurer issuing a policy providing crop insurance coverage, other than federal crop insurance pursuant to 7 U.S.C. Section 1501, et seq., for tobacco crops grown in this state against loss or damage due to wind, hail, or both shall make available such coverage for a term extending until such time as the tobacco crop is harvested, either as a part of or as an optional endorsement to such policy of crop insurance.

## History

Amended by 2019 Ga. Laws 140,§ 108, eff. 7/1/2019.
