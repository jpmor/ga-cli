# § 33-13A-7. Application of other statutory provisions

(a) Code Section 33-14-76 is not applicable to a reorganization or merger pursuant to this chapter.
(b) The demutualization of a mutual insurance holding company is subject to the requirements of Code Section 33-14-76.

## History

Added by 2015 Ga. Laws 120,§ 3, eff. 7/1/2015.
