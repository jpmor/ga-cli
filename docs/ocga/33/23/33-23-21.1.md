# § 33-23-21.1. Licensing for educational borrowers in default

The Commissioner shall not refuse to issue nor suspend or revoke a license of an applicant for or holder of a license because he or she has been certified by any entity of the federal government for nonpayment or default or breach of a repayment or service obligation under any federal educational loan, loan repayment, or service conditional scholarship program.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 399,§ 2-34, eff. 6/30/2024.
Added by 2019 Ga. Laws 146,§ 1-9, eff. 7/1/2019.
