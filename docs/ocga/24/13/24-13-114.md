# § 24-13-114. Service of foreign subpoena

A subpoena issued by the clerk of superior court under Code Section 24-13-112 or 24-13-113 shall be served in compliance with Code Section 24-13-23 and shall be served within a reasonable time prior to the appearance required by such subpoena.

## History

Added by 2012 Ga. Laws 643,§ 2-1, eff. 1/1/2013.
