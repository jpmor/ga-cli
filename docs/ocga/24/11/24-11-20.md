# § 24-11-20. Establishment of lost office papers

(a) Upon the loss of any original pleading, declaration, bill of indictment, special presentment, accusation, or other office paper, a duplicate may be established instanter on motion.
(b) As used in this article, the term "office paper" means the instrument upon which a proceeding has been brought after the case has gone to trial.

## History

Added by 2011 Ga. Laws 52,§ 2, eff. 1/1/2013.
