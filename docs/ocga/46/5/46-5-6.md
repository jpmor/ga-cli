# § 46-5-6. Exclusive power and authority of the Public Service Commission to prescribe rules and regulations regarding public pay telephones

The Public Service Commission shall have the exclusive power and authority to prescribe rules and regulations for the operation, maintenance, location, and deployment of public pay telephones within this state.

## History

Added by 2001 Ga. Laws 262, § 3, eff. 7/1/2001.
