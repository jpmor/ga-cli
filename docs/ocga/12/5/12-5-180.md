# § 12-5-180. Duty of permit applicants and holders to supply information as required by director

(a) Any applicant for a permit whose application is pending final consideration shall upon request of the director provide such additional information as may be necessary to complete final disposition of the application.
(b) Any permit holder shall on request of the director furnish such information as is reasonably necessary and pertinent to enable the director to discharge his duties under this part.
