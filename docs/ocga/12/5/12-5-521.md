# § 12-5-521. Definitions

(1) "Director" means the director of the Environmental Protection Division of the Department of Natural Resources.
(2) "Division" means the Environmental Protection Division of the Department of Natural Resources.
(3) "Water Council" means the Water Council created by Code Section 12-5-524.

## History

Amended by 2005 Ga. Laws 19,§ 12, eff. 4/7/2005.
Added by 2004 Ga. Laws 571, § 2, eff. 5/13/2004.
Former § 12-5-521 was repealed by 2004 Ga. Laws 571, § 2, eff. 5/13/2004.
