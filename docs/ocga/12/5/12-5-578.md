# § 12-5-578. Adjoining counties or municipalities application to be added to the district area

Any county or municipality adjoining a member county or municipality shall be added to the district area upon the application of such entity to be included in the district by resolution of its governing authority and upon approval of the director.

## History

Added by 2001 Ga. Laws 27, § 1, eff. 5/1/2001.
