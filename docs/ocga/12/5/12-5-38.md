# § 12-5-38. Aid to pollution control and surface-water management - Management by division of federal construction grants program

The division is authorized to manage the construction grants program as set forth in Title II of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act Amendments of 1972 (P.L. 92-500), 33 U.S.C. Section 1281, et seq., as amended by the Clean Water Act of 1977 (P.L. 95-217), or as may hereafter be amended, upon receiving delegation of such program from the administrator of the federal Environmental Protection Agency. The division shall manage such program in accordance with the requirements and conditions set forth in Title II of the Federal Water Pollution Control Act Amendments of 1972, as amended.
