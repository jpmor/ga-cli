# § 12-3-10.1. Directing persons to leave parks, historic sites, or recreation areas; penalty

(a) Any person who violates any rules and regulations adopted pursuant to paragraph (1) of subsection (a) of Code Section 12-3-9 and who refuses to cease such violation after notice may be directed to leave the park, historic site, or recreational area on which the violation occurs. A person shall have no legal authority, right, or privilege to remain upon a state park, historic site, or recreational area after receiving such a direction.
(b) Any person violating the provisions of this Code section shall be guilty of a misdemeanor.
