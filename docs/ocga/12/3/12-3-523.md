# § 12-3-523. Corporate purpose and general nature of business

(1) Coordinating with the Classic Center Authority and entities dedicated to archiving and collecting music history and artifacts to house the Georgia Music Hall of Fame;
(2) Operating, advertising, and promoting the Georgia Music Hall of Fame; and
(3) Promoting music events throughout the state.

## History

Amended by 2022 Ga. Laws 844,§ 4, eff. 7/1/2022.
