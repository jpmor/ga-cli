# § 12-6-46. State Board of Registration for Foresters - Meetings; officers

The board shall hold meetings as necessary. The board shall elect or appoint annually a chairman and a vice-chairman. The division director of the professional licensing boards division, as provided in Chapter 1 of Title 43, shall serve as secretary of the board in the same manner as provided by Code Sections 43-1-1 and 43-1-2.

## History

Amended by 2001 Ga. Laws 2, § 12, eff. 2/12/2001.
