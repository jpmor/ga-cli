# § 20-2-209. Financial literacy endorsement for educators

(a) No later than December 30, 2023, the Professional Standards Commission shall create a financial literacy endorsement for teachers trained in the content standards for financial literacy adopted by the State Board of Education pursuant to Code Section 20-2-149.4.
(b) The Professional Standards Commission shall establish measures to assess the fidelity of teacher training and implementation for teachers who receive the financial literacy endorsement.

## History

Added by 2022 Ga. Laws 723,§ 1-3, eff. 4/28/2022.
