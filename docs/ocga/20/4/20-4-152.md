# § 20-4-152. [Repealed effective 7/1/2027] Utilization of the High-demand Career List

The board and the Office of Workforce Development shall utilize the High-demand Career List provided for in Code Section 34-14-3 for purposes of the High Demand Apprenticeship Program.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 554,§ 2-2, eff. 7/1/2024.
Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 560,§ 1-2, eff. 7/1/2024.
Added by 2022 Ga. Laws 728,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2022.
