# § 20-14-6. Selection of personnel to support alliance

All departments, boards, and offices represented on the alliance, the Office of Planning and Budget, and the Office of Student Achievement shall select and appoint such personnel as each such department, board, or office shall determine to be necessary to support the alliance in the performance of its duties, with the approval of the alliance. Such personnel shall serve at the direction of the alliance. Payment for all costs and salaries of such personnel shall come from funds appropriated to the respective departments, boards, and offices.

## History

Amended by 2004 Ga. Laws 449, § 22, eff. 5/4/2004.
