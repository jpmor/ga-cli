# § 20-2B-1. Definitions

(1) "Board of directors" or "board" means the board of directors of the education savings authority.
(2) "Commission" means the Georgia Student Finance Commission.
(3) "Director" means a member of the board of directors.
(4) "Education savings authority" means the Georgia Education Savings Authority created by this article.

## History

Added by 2024 Ga. Laws 457,§ 1-1, eff. 4/23/2024.
