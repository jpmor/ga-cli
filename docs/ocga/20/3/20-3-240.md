# § 20-3-240. Budget requests

The commission shall submit its regular and supplemental budget requests to the Governor at such times and in such form and manner as required by state law; provided, however, that the commission shall, in accordance with Code Section 50-4-3, include in its budget requests the budget request, if any, of the authority as prepared and submitted to the commission by the authority.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 399,§ 2-13, eff. 6/30/2024.
