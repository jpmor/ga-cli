# § 20-3-318. Authority administration fund

The authority shall maintain an administration fund as provided for in this Code section to which shall be credited state moneys, federal moneys, other moneys, and earned income of the authority available for and determined by the authority to be applied for the purpose of paying administration costs of the authority. The authority is authorized to pay from the fund administration costs incurred by the authority and to pay to the commission such amounts as may be necessary to cover administration costs of the authority paid or to be paid by the commission for or on behalf of the authority.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 399,§ 2-17, eff. 6/30/2024.
