# § 20-3-518.3. Purchases of loans made for educational purposes to students who have completed a veterinary medical degree program

(a) The board may provide for the purchase of loans made to students who are residents of Georgia for educational purposes who have completed a program of study in the field of doctor of veterinary medicine or its equivalent and are authorized to practice veterinary medicine in this state, with services in the form of the practice of veterinary medicine while residing in this state to be rendered as consideration for such loan purchases.
(b) Only persons whose veterinary medicine practices in this state include food animal specialties shall be eligible for loan purchases under this part.

## History

Added by 2011 Ga. Laws 80,§ 2, eff. 7/1/2011.
