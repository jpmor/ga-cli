# § 37-3-208.1. One-time provisional license

Between July 1, 2022, and June 30, 2025, the department shall be authorized to grant a one-time provisional license for an adult residential mental health program to an existing licensed personal care home that substantially complies with the requirements of this article for a period not to extend beyond June 30, 2025.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 475,§ 3, eff. 4/23/2024.
Added by 2022 Ga. Laws 829,§ 1, eff. 7/1/2022.
