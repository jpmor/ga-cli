# § 37-1-7. Funding for new co-responder programs

The state shall provide funding for a minimum of five new co-responder programs established pursuant to this title. Each such program shall have a minimum of one co-responder team.

## History

Amended by 2024 Ga. Laws 701,§ 6, eff. 7/1/2024.
Added by 2022 Ga. Laws 587,§ 4-7, eff. 7/1/2022.
